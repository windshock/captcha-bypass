How AI Dismantled the CAPTCHA — Why Your Login Security Is a $0 Automatic Door

  This video analyzes why CAPTCHA is no longer a security barrier,
  based on a real bypass proof-of-concept and a deep research report.

  Using just three free open-source tools — Playwright, Whisper, and Page-Agent —
  we demonstrate a pipeline that defeats audio CAPTCHAs at zero cost,
  and examine how LLM vision agents that "read" the DOM
  have rendered every CAPTCHA modality — image, text, slider, and behavioral analysis —
  effectively useless.

  The core issue isn't technology — it's economics.
  We dissect the extreme asymmetry where defenders invest millions
  while attackers break through for the price of a cup of coffee ($0.50 per 1,000 solves).

  The second half presents a 5-layer post-CAPTCHA defense blueprint
  and explains why Passkey/FIDO2 — eliminating passwords entirely —
  is the only viable endgame.

  This is a security research video, not a guide for attacking any service.
  All analysis is based on published research and open-source tools.
